Basic Digital Skills for Secondary School Students

Ages 14-19 JS3 - SS3 4 Weeks
3 sessions per week • 1.5 hours per session
💻

Week 1: Computers & Internet

  • Day 1: What is a computer? Hardware vs Software, Basic parts, Turning on/off safely
  • Day 2: Internet basics & safety, Gmail, Google Workspace (Docs, Drive)
  • Day 3: Word Processing: Typing, editing, formatting, saving, printing
📊

Week 2: Spreadsheet Basics

  • Day 4: Spreadsheet environment, rows, columns, cells, entering data
  • Day 5: Formatting, merging, formulas (=SUM, =AVERAGE, =COUNT), auto-fill
  • Day 6: Real-life tasks: score sheet, timetable, budget tracker
🎨

Week 3: Graphics Design

  • Day 7: What is graphic design? Canva/PowerPoint overview, templates
  • Day 8: School ID card, event flyer, timetable design
  • Day 9: Presentations: slides, text, images, animations, presenting
🖨️

Week 4: Photocopy & Integration

  • Day 10: Photocopy machine parts, making copies, loading paper, clearing jams
  • Day 11: Final project integration - combining all skills learned
  • Day 12: Student presentations and course completion certificates
